Forgotten Device Archive

This place is meant to document any electronic device that didn't quite stand the test of time.

We are talking about pieces of hardware about which you can barely find something on the internet. There is no available user manual, no selling offers, no known dedicated forums. They are either possibly brandless, Chinese originated, or their manufacturer website had shut down many years ago. This is the sad story of devices that once were always present in any careless kid's pockets making their life beautiful, but yet no one talks about.
